About Event

Kickboxing is returning to Windsor! Join us on September 20, 2024, at the WFCU Banquet Hall for the KINGSS - King Striking Series: Live K1 Kickboxing! Doors open at 6:00 PM, with the first fight kicking off at 7:00 PM.

 

This thrilling event, sanctioned by WAKO Canada, will feature some of the top fighters in the region, showcasing their speed, power, and precision in the ring. Whether you're a seasoned fight fan or new to the sport, this is an evening you won't want to miss. Witness the intensity of K1 kickboxing live and be part of an unforgettable night of martial arts excellence!

Tickets are available now! General admission can be purchased on Eventbrite, while VIP Seats, VIP Ringside Seats, and VIP Tables are on sale now for those looking to enjoy the action up close. To secure VIP seating, contact us on Instagram or reach out to your favorite fighter on the card.

 

Follow Us on Instagram: @kingstrikingseries
